Player Controls:
- W: Move Forward
- S: Move Backward
- A: Move Left
- D: Move Right
- Space: Attack
- ESC: Quit Game

Enemy AI:
- Automatically detects player within detection range (yellow sphere in editor)
- Chases player when detected
- Attacks when player is within attack range (red sphere in editor)
- Returns to chase if player moves out of attack range
- Returns to idle if player moves out of detection range
